Synopsis

In Stop Overthinking, Nick Trenton offers concise, practical techniques that help readers interrupt runaway thought patterns and rediscover calm. Grounded in cognitive-behavioural strategies and written with a warm, editorial voice, this book guides you through 23 simple practices to reduce worry and act with clarity.
